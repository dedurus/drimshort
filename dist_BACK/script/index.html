Script & Rough Cut Corner

Drim Short's Script Corner and Rough Cut Corner platforms were born as a result of our personal experience as filmmakers and screenwriters, and the journey our stories take from the initial idea for the script, to the final cut of the film. It is not always easy, and not everyone wants to participate in script development workshops and rough cut labs, and it is even more difficult to be part of the best workshops around Europe, but anyone who writes screenplays or is part of the editing process, is aware of the need for objective criticism, advice and suggestion from other screenwriters, script doctors and editing mentors, ie. fresh eyes who will see the project without being involved in it.

Script Corner and Rough Cut Corner are platforms that in their team will include younger and more experienced screenwriters, directors and script mentors, who will guide you through the process of developing and writing your scripts, as well as through the process of creating dramaturgy in editing. Given the current situation and disability for normal physical communication, online consultations are one of the benefits of this time we are living, because from the comfort zone of our "rooms where we work" we will have the opportunity for professional support and maximum commitment of mentors in one-on-one sessions.

Team

Members of our team are Tamara Kotevska (director), Wim Vanacker (screenwriter, script doctor) and Jakov Poposki (screenwriter). We hope that the team will soon expand with other professionals in the field of screenwriting and editing. All members of our team have had or are currently working on serious professional projects, collaborating with top professionals from the film industry in Europe in the process of writing or editing, and some of them already have serious experience as script doctors and script mentors. The team members know each other and have previously collaborated or helped each other with their projects, so each of them will approach your work in the same principle.
